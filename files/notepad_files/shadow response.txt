"Is there someone there?" you call out to the darkness. The whispers in your head intensify and your vision goes black. 
You hear a voice. "There are many things here, outsider" the voice says before pausing. 
"But what is it that you are doing among them?" To this you can only muster a weak, "I don't know". The response is something 
sinister that might be a chuckle. "I have a question for you, outsider..." it says, so close you feel you could touch it. 

"Are you afraid of me?"
