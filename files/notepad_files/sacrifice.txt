You follow the figures outside into a sunny pasture ending in a cliff over the sea. You are overjoyed to see sunlight, but as you continue to follow, you begin to feel that something is wrong. They seem to have formed a semi-circle around you, and they just keep heading toward the cliff. You reach the edge, and then turn and realize that they have all stopped behind you. "We prayed for a sacrifice, and this holy place delivered", she says, as the rest of the congregation begins to chant in another language.
